# Privacy Policy for Simple Volume Booster - Boost and Control the Dynamics

**Effective Date:** April 25, 2026

## 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy applies to the **Simple Volume Booster - Boost and Control the Dynamics** browser extension (hereinafter referred to as "SVB"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. 

## 2. Data Collection and Usage
* **Telemetry:** We may collect anonymous usage data such as boost and dynamics settings to enhance the performance and features of our extension.
* **No Personal Information:** We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, email address, IP address, or any other identifying information.
* **No Analytics:** We do not use Google Analytics or any other tracking software to monitor how you use SVB.
* **Local Operation:** All audio processing and volume boosting occurs entirely locally on your device, within your browser.

## 3. Permissions
To function correctly, SVB requires certain browser permissions (such as accessing the active tab or audio context). These permissions are strictly used for the technical operation of SVB. 

* **Why we need them:** We need permission to attach an audio node to the current webpage to amplify and control the sound. 
* **How they are used:** These permissions are used locally in your browser in real-time. We do not use these permissions to read your browsing history, intercept your passwords, or record your audio.

## 4. Third-Party Services
SVB does not utilize any third-party services, APIs, or external servers. Everything needed for SVB to run is contained within the installation package.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Because SVB does not collect personal information, any changes will likely reflect updates to browser store policies or new features. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
